# Financial Literacy & Investment Choices of College Students

## Overview
This research project analyzes financial literacy levels and investment behaviour among students at St Aloysius (Deemed to be University), Mangalore.

## Objective
To understand students' financial knowledge, saving behaviour, and investment patterns.

## Methodology
- Survey of 32 university students
- 14-question questionnaire
- Descriptive analysis of responses

## Key Findings
- 59% of students maintain a personal budget
- Over 80% save regularly or occasionally
- 56% have already invested money
- Internet is the main source of financial knowledge
